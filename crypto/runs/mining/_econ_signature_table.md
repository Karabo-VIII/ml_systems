## Honest notes on apparent mismatches

- **Ljung-Box(ret) p=0 on all 4** looks like a DISAGREE vs section II's "direction unpredictable", but at n~12-14k the test rejects on the economically negligible bid-ask-bounce AC1 (|AC1|~0.02-0.03, negative for BTC/ETH/SOL). That tiny negative AC *is* section II's documented "micro-scale negative AC (bid-ask bounce, Roll)" stylized fact -- it is NOT directional predictability. Flagged AGREE-IN-SPIRIT. (Ljung-Box on |ret| / ret^2 is the genuine vol-clustering signal: p=0, AGREE.)
- **GJR leverage gamma is MIXED across assets**: BTC +0.017 (mild equity-sign), SOL +0.010 (mild equity-sign), ETH +0.009 (~absent), DOGE -0.049 (inverted/crypto-sign). Section II tags BTC leverage inverted/absent but explicitly marks it [UNCERTAIN -- active debate] and period/coin-dependent. The spread we measure (mild-positive majors -> inverted meme) is consistent with that uncertainty, not a bug.
- **GARCH persistence rounds to exactly 1.0** for all 4 (alpha+beta near-integrated). Half-life is therefore reported as None (shocks effectively never decay) rather than a spurious huge number.
- **GARCH nu (Student-t dof) ~3.0-4.0** for all 4 -- directly matches section II's "GARCH-Student-t innovations fit nu~3-4" and corroborates the Hill tail-index (alpha~nu for a t-distribution).
- **DOGE is the meme outlier**: excess kurtosis 108 (vs 9-19 majors), positive skew +3.36, heaviest tails (Hill alpha 1.96-2.45), and the only inverted leverage -- the section-II meme archetype, quantified.
