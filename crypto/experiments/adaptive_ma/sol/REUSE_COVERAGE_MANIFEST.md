## (a) Capturable catalogs — ARE they reusable pre-computed oracle ceilings?

### `data/processed/capturable_4h_catalog.parquet`  ✅ REUSABLE as the **4h** oracle ceiling
- rows: **665,512** · cols: 10 · assets: **87** (incl. SOL/BTC/ETH/DOGE/PEPE) · span: **2020-01-03 → 2026-05-15**
- cols: `asset, ts, close, max_gain_12bar, max_loss_12bar, max_gain_18bar, max_loss_18bar, win_3pct_12bar, win_5pct_18bar, split`
- **Nature = pure forward-looking ORACLE target catalog**: for every 4h bar, the realized forward
  max-gain / max-loss over the next **12 bars (48h)** and **18 bars (72h)**, plus binary win labels
  (≥3% within 12 bars; ≥5% within 18 bars). NO input features — it is the *ceiling definition* (what
  was capturable), not a strategy. `split` (train/val/...) present → honest eval ready.
- **Alignment check**: catalog rows per asset ≈ chimera `4h` bar counts (SOL 12,403 vs 12,482; BTC
  13,918 vs 13,996; ETH 13,871 vs 13,951; DOGE 12,289 vs 12,368) → built off the same `v51_4h` bars,
  minus the trailing forward-window truncation. → **directly reusable as the per-bar 4h capture ceiling.**
- ⚠️ Caveat: `ts` is NOT on clean 4-hour clock marks (e.g. 19:50:16.149) — the underlying `v51_4h`
  bars are event/last-tick-stamped, not wall-clock 4h. Label "4h" = the chimera 4h cadence, not literal.

### `data/processed/capturable_win_catalog.parquet`  ✅ REUSABLE but it is the **DAILY (1d/3d)** ceiling, NOT 4h
- rows: **111,345** · cols: 14 · assets: **87** (all 5 focus present) · granularity: **daily** (`date` col)
- cols: `asset, date, close, high_3d_fwd, low_3d_fwd, max_gain_3d, max_loss_3d, max_gain_1d, max_gain_2d,
  win_3pct, win_5pct, win_7pct, win_10pct, split`
- **Nature = daily forward-3-day ORACLE catalog**: per day, forward 3-day high/low, max gain/loss over
  1d/2d/3d, win labels at 3/5/7/10%. Rows per asset ≈ chimera `1d` counts (SOL 2,069 vs 2,083).
- Use it as the **daily/3-day capture ceiling**, not the 4h one. Don't conflate the two files.

**Bottom line (a):** both are genuine, reusable, pre-computed oracle/capturable per-move catalogs over
87 assets with train/val splits. `capturable_4h_catalog` = 4h ceiling; `capturable_win_catalog` = daily.
No re-computation needed to set either ceiling.
